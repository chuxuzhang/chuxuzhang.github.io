About

I am currently a PhD candidate under the supervision of Professor Nitesh V. Chawla in University of Notre Dame. I received B.E. degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China under the supervision of Professor Tao Zhou. My general research interests include applied machine learning and deep learning, especially for applications in graphs, recommendation, and natural language modeling. Recently, I focus on graph neural networks, knowledge bases, and few-shot learning.
