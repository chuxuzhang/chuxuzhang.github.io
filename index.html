I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Brandeis University. My research interests are artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data mining, and their applications to social good problems. Recently, I focus on developing resource efficient (data, model, knowledge) and trustworthy (robust, fair, interpretable) graph learning theories and algorithms. I also use AI to improve public health, provide food/nutrition service, solve social/information/recommendation problems, and advance interdisciplinary studies.
