About UConn: The University of Connecticut (UConn) is a public research university located in Storrs, Connecticut. Recognized as a Public Ivy school, UConn is classified among 'R1: Doctoral Universities – Very high research activity.' It ranks 58th in the 2024 US News Best National University Rankings. The campus is approximately 1.5 hours from Boston and 2.5 hours from New York City.

About UConn SoC: The School of Computing (SoC), formerly the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, at UConn is rapidly growing. Most faculty of SoC are NSF CAREER awardees. In particular, it has some strong faculty in AI, machine learning, and data science. Many graduates have secured tenure-track positions at reputable institutions in the U.S. (e.g., Stevens Institute of Technology, UT Arlington) and roles as research scientists at top industry companies (e.g., Google, Amazon, Meta, Microsoft). The SoC ranks around 60 in CSRankings.
